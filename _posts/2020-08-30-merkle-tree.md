Merkle trees provide a time and space-efficient way of hashing nested values. Merkle tree nodes contain the
value of the hashes if its children, until the individual leaf nodes.

A simple gist can be found [here](https://gist.github.com/ilknarf/e442135939bb9d56664d18f41a5ba03c.)
